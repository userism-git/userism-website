All power to the users,
one bright future for all.

An open project by the Userist Collective.

Scroll to find more

Here's why:

User and the end of work

With the advent of digital, everything changes. Work, intended as physical and mental effort to produce value, is slowly replaced by automation. Only human needs and desires remain the same, but freed from most physical constraints. Users live freely in a truly human age.

Consumers and the digital gesture

As tasks get automated and products generated, a simple gesture becomes the source of power. It's the digital gesture, the moment where human intention meets digital potential. Without it, no technology has meaning, and no product carries any value. Consumption, therefore, becomes far more relevant than production.

Courage and beautiful future

Humans are at their most powerful in the digital age. Today, there are better chances of a bright future for everyone than ever in history. However, digital control is unequally distributed across countries and societies, and this is an obstacle to potential progress. That's why we believe it should be users' right to own it, not be afraid of it, or of its consequences. As userocracy is put into place, benefits will equally unfold for everyone.
