About us

Martina: Design and object-based research.
Renato: International relations and digital power research.

Together we reflect on the digital consumer and the evolving notion of userism. We launched the Userist Collective.

After meeting in Shanghai we began to think together about the global economy, arriving at an important intution: at its core are the users. Since then, our research has aimed to explore their potential, driven by the belief in collective ownership of digital data for the benefit of all.
