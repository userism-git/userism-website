About us

Martina: Design and object-based research.
Renato: International relations and digital power research.
Together we founded the Userist Collective, to reflect on the digital consumer and the evolving notion of userism.

Our vision

We believe that digital means shifted wealth production from work to consumption— and that's where the real conflict lies. This happened as digital created a powerful mass of users, though for now fragmented, which is the core of contemporary economy.

Our work and research aim to explore the potential of users, how they could organize to resist digital inequality, and how they could take ownership of the future on behalf of the people. It is a big, difficult task—but we are confident it is the right one.

We move forward without nostalgia for a past that is gone, and without fear of a technology that, if guided by the people, could deliver the most "human" century the world has ever seen.

How to join us

This is an open collective. If you'd like to join just contact us.

We will need to know: your name; your email; the city you live in.

You can help us: writing articles; contributing to research; managing communication; expanding the network; and whatever you think might be useful.
